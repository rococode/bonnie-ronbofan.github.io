About Ronbo Fan

Ronbo Fan is a Technical Program Manager at Citrix, where he leads cross-functional engineering initiatives that deliver secure, scalable cloud workspace solutions to millions of enterprise users worldwide. With a foundation in software engineering and deep expertise in hybrid multi-cloud architecture, Ronbo bridges the gap between complex technical execution and strategic product delivery.

His work centers on orchestrating the development and release of Citrix DaaS (Desktop as a Service) and VDI (Virtual Desktop Infrastructure) platforms — technologies that power the modern distributed workforce. Ronbo is known for bringing structured rigor to ambiguous, high-stakes programs while maintaining the velocity that competitive markets demand.

Based in the San Francisco Bay Area, Ronbo combines hands-on technical fluency with program leadership to ensure that globally distributed teams ship reliable, high-availability enterprise software on schedule and at scale.
